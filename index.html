What are these weird project names?

I'm a huge fan of Warhammer and reading books, and the Space Marine Battles series of books are the first Warhammer ones I've read. When I'm doing a major project I pick a major character from those books for the project's name. This includes Reclusiarch Merek Grimaldus of the Black Templars, or Captain Cato Sicarius of the Ultramarines.
